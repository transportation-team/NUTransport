Tips & Tricks

  1. To get real-time arrival information, text “CTABUS” or “CTATRAIN,” followed by a bus stop number or ’L’ station name, to 41411.
  2. If you plan to use the CTA a lot, you can buy one-day, 3-day, 7-day, or 30-day passes to save money.
  3. The CTA uses Ventra as it’s payment system. You can get Ventra Cards at any Ventra Vending Machine in ‘L’ stations, Ventra Retailers around town, online or by phone.
  4. Ventra Cards cost $5, but the cost is returned to you as value if you register your card online (by computer or smartphone) or by phone within 90 days.
  5. Visitors can obtain a temporary Ventra ticket (paper) from the Ventra fare machines at Midway and O’hare.
  6. The basic ‘L’ or subway fare is $2.50 ($5.00 from O’hare). Free transfers between all ‘L’ routes are available at designated locations.
  7. You cannot pay your fare in cash to board the L but you can to board the buses.
  8. If you transfer to a bus it costs $.25. A transfer is good for up to 2 additional rides within 2 hours.
  9. The basic bus fare is $2.25. You can also buy a transfer. It will deduct the .25 from your card when you board another bus.
